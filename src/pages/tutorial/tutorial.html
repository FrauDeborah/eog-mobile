Easyah makes it easier to collaborate with other people on big ideas. It uses the idea of paying-it-forward to make it work.

This is an early TEST version, so let me know your thoughts at info@easyah.io!

 Quickly, there are a three concepts you need to understand to use Easyah.


Promises
..Offers you make to do something for someone.

Points
..Increase your ability to request a Promise from someone else. You receive points from other users, when they appreciate something you did, either one at a time (slooow), or in bunches when you fulfill a promise (faster).

Recommendations
..Let others know where your points came from. Some promises will require a recommendation before they can be requested.

 As you might expect, you can search for..

people, to send them points, or give them your recommendation,

or
people's promises, to request them, if you have the points!


TIP! Try searching for 'Denver'.

 To get started, click on the menu button in the upper left.

There will be a menu option, Your Promises. You can create your Promises here.

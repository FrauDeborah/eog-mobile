Welcome! Easyah is your gateway to a network of people, doing their part to improve their corner of the world.

Quickly, there are three things you need to understand to use Easyah.

  • Dreams / These are tasks that people want to accomplish.

  • Promises / These are offers to do something for someone.

  • Points / These allow one person to ask another person to fulfll a promise they made.

So, in a nutshell, you define your Dreams, and then you search for other users who have made Promises which can help you. These Promises will require points. When you have enough points, you can make a request for a person to fulfill the Promise they made, for you.

Sometimes, you will need to be the person making the Promise for other people, as opposed to asking someone else to do theirs for you. This is the primary way you will receive points. When you fulfill your Promise, you get a number of points in return. You decide how many that is.

 So, Easyah, is like life!

Sometimes you are doing things for other people, sometimes they are doing things for you. If you do both of those here on Easyah, you will be helping our mission: to make Good Things(tm) happen.

Join us online, in our Facebook group! Search for 'easyah.io'.

To get started now,
  • Click on the box at the top of the screen to go to your Profile. From there, you can create your Promises, and Dreams.
  • Or, you may want to search for other people, and see their Promises and Dreams. Search by tapping the magnifying glass in the upper right corner of the home page.

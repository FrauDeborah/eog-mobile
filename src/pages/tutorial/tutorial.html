Welcome! Easyah is your gateway to a network of people, doing their part to improve their corner of the world.

Quickly, there are three things you need to understand to use Easyah.

  • Promises / These are offers you make to do something for someone.

  • Points / These allow one person to ask another person to fulfill a promise they made.

So, in a nutshell, you create a profile, and then you search for Promises by other users who can help you. These Promises will require points. When you have earned enough points, you can make a request for a person to fulfill the Promise they made, for you.

Sometimes, instead of asking people to fulfill a promise for you, you will need to be the person making and fulfilling Promises for other people. This is the primary way you will receive points. When you fulfill your Promise, you get a number of points in return. You decide how many that is.

 Sometimes you are doing things for other people, sometimes they are doing things for you. If you do both of those here on Easyah, you will be helping our mission: to make Good Things(tm) happen.

Join us online, in our Facebook group! Search for 'easyah.io'.

To get started now,
  • Click on your contact info at the top of the screen to go to your Profile. From there, you can create your Promises.
  • Or, you may want to search for other people, and see their Promises. Search by tapping the magnifying glass in the upper right corner of the home page.

Welcome! Easyah is a tool for paying-it-forward. With it, you can collaborate with others who are also paying-it-forward, and maximize your impact.

Quickly, there are a two concepts you need to understand to use Easyah.

  • Promises / These are offers you make to do something for someone.

  • Points / These allow one person to ask another person to fulfill a promise they made.

So, in a nutshell, you create a profile, and then you search for Promises by other users who can help you. These Promises will require points.

You earn points, primarily, by paying-it-forward, fulfilling your Promises for others. When you have earned enough points, you can make a request for a person to fulfill the Promise they made, for you.

Sometimes you are doing things for other people, sometimes they are doing things for you. Our goal is to match the people who can-do, with those who need something done.

As they work together, more Good happens. The more people work together and help each other, the more the world will change for the better.

It starts with you, making a promise. It starts, with Easyah.


Join us online, in our Facebook group! Search for 'easyah.io'.

To get started now,
  • Click on your name at the top of the screen to go to your Profile. From there, you can create your Promises.
  • Or, you may want to search, and find what other people have promised. You can also search for a specific user. Find a friend, and send them a point!

Welcome! Easyah is a tool for paying-it-forward. With it, you can collaborate with others who are also paying-it-forward, and maximize your impact.

This is an early TEST version, so things may not work, or could be klunky to use. Let me know your thoughts at info@easyah.io!

Quickly, there are a three concepts you need to understand to use Easyah.

  • Promises / ..Offers you make to do something for someone.

  • Points / ..Increase your ability to request a Promise from someone else. You receive points from other users, when they appreciate something you did, either one at a time (slooow), or in bunches when you fulfill a promise (faster).

  • Recommendations / ..Let others know where your points came from. Some promises will require a recommendation before you can request them.

 At the top left of the Home screen, is a magnifying glass. Use this to Search for..
  • other people, to send them points, or give your recommendation,
  • or other people's promises, to request them, if you can!
TIP! Try searching for 'Denver'.

To get started, click on your name at the top of the Home screen. This will take you to your Profile. You can create your Promises here. Promises are anonymous! No other user will know it is yours, until you accept their request. If you deny it, you remain anonymous.
